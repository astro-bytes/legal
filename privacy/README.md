# Privacy Policy  
**Effective Date:** 8/18/25

Sim Racing Setups (“the App”) is developed by **Astrobytes** (“we,” “our,” or “us”).  
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.  

## 1. Information We Collect  
We may collect:  
- **Account information** (such as email or username, if provided by you).  
- **App usage data** (e.g., features used, performance metrics).  
- **User-generated content** (setups, notes, lap times, and related racing data).  

## 2. How We Use Information  
We use the information to:  
- Provide, operate, and improve the App’s features.  
- Store and manage your setups and racing data in our database.  
- Ensure proper functionality across devices.  
- Analyze trends and performance (anonymous and aggregated).  

## 3. Data Storage  
- All setups, lap times, and related data are stored securely in our database.  
- Data may be backed up using third-party cloud infrastructure.  
- We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.  

## 4. Third-Party Services  
The App may use third-party services such as:  
- **Cloud hosting providers** (for database storage and backups).  
- **Analytics providers** (to understand how the App is used).  
- **Apple App Store services** (for distribution and analytics).  

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy.  

## 5. Your Rights  
You have the right to:  
- Request access to the data associated with your account.  
- Request deletion of your data from our database.  
- Stop using the App, which will stop new data collection.  

## 6. Children’s Privacy  
The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.  

## 7. Changes to This Policy  
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on our website.
