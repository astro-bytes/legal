# Privacy Policy
**Effective Date:** 8/18/25

Applications developed by **Astrobytes** (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile, desktop, and web applications created and published by Astrobytes (each referred to as “the App”).

## 1. Information We Collect
Depending on the App and how you use it, we may collect:
- **Account information** (such as email address or username, if you choose to provide one).
- **App usage data** (including features used, interactions, and performance metrics).
- **User-generated content** (such as notes, configurations, files, data entries, or other content you create within the App).

## 2. How We Use Information
We use collected information to:
- Provide, operate, and improve App functionality.
- Store and manage your data and content.
- Sync data across devices, where applicable.
- Monitor performance and analyze usage trends in an anonymous and aggregated manner.

## 3. Data Storage
- User data is stored securely in our databases.
- Data may be backed up using third-party cloud infrastructure.
- We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

## 4. Third-Party Services
Astrobytes apps may rely on third-party services, including but not limited to:
- **Cloud hosting and storage providers** (for data storage and backups).
- **Analytics services** (to understand app usage and improve performance).
- **App distribution platforms** such as the Apple App Store or Google Play.

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Request access to data associated with your use of an App.
- Request deletion of your data, where applicable.
- Discontinue use of the App at any time, which will stop further data collection.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
Astrobytes apps are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be made available within the App or on our official website.
